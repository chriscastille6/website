# Research Assessment Library

Our assessment library provides validated psychological instruments designed for academic research and organizational applications. Each assessment combines rigorous scientific methodology with modern, user-friendly interfaces.

## Available Assessments

### Current Collection
- **Occupational Fit Assessment** - Adaptive conjoint analysis for workplace preferences
- **Research Personality Inventory** - Big Five personality measurement with workplace applications  
- **People Analyst Fit Assessment** - Comprehensive fit assessment across five key domains (Liu et al., 2024)
- **Emotional Intelligence Assessment** - Four-branch ability-based EI measurement (framework ready)

### Coming Soon
Additional assessments are in development to expand our research capabilities and organizational applications.

## Features

- **Research-Grade Methodology** - Based on established psychological theories and validated instruments
- **Modern Interfaces** - Responsive web applications that work on all devices
- **Anonymous Data Collection** - Privacy-first approach with optional consent for identification
- **Academic Integration** - Designed for peer-reviewed research and organizational consulting
